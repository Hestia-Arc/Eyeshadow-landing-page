EYESHADOWS

For Popping Eyes

Eyeshadow is a coloured cosmetic applied primarily to the eyelids to make the wearer's eyes stand out or look more attractive. Eye shadow can also be applied under eyes ,cheeks or to brow bones.
Eye shadow can :

  • Add depth and dimension to one's eyes.
  • Complement one's eye color.
  • Make one's eyes appear larger.
  • Draw attention to the eyes.

Eye shadow comes in many different colors and textures. It is usually made from a powder but can also be found in liquid, pencil, cream or mousse form.
